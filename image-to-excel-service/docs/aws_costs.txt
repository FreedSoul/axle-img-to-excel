AWS COST & REVENUE ANALYSIS

Comparison of the implemented strategy (Bedrock) vs the future optimization (Gemini).

## Strategy B: "The Smart Balance" (Current Implementation)
**Stack**: AWS (S3 + Lambda + Bedrock Llama 3.2)

**Cost per 1,000 Images:**
- **Model (Bedrock Llama 3.2 11B)**: ~$0.40 - $0.50 (varies by resolution/tokens)
- **Infrastructure (Lambda + S3)**: ~$0.20
- **Data Transfer**: $0.00 (Internal AWS network traffic is free)
- **TOTAL COST**: ~$0.60 - $0.70 per 1,000 images.

**Profit Model:**
- **Market Price**: Charging customer $0.03 per image.
- **Revenue (1k images)**: $30.00
- **Profit**: ~$29.30 (Margin ~97%)

---

## Strategy C: "The Profit Maximizer" (Future Goal)
**Stack**: Hybrid (Cloudflare R2 + Google Gemini 1.5/2.0 Flash)

**Cost per 1,000 Images:**
- **Model (Gemini Flash)**: ~$0.02 - $0.10
- **Infrastructure (R2 Storage)**: Negligible
- **Data Transfer (Egress)**: $0.00 (Cloudflare R2 has zero egress fees)
- **TOTAL COST**: ~$0.05 - $0.15 per 1,000 images.

**Comparison**:
The Bedrock path costs ~$0.55 more per 1,000 images but saves significant development time and complexity. It is the ideal MVP starting point.
Switching to the "Profit Path" becomes valuable when volume exceeds ~10,000-50,000 images/month, where that $0.55 difference adds up to meaningful savings.
